## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR DRAFT ENGINE

1. **Crow is the #1 priority** — 96.5% presence, must be dealt with every single game
2. **Bull + Glowbert are perma-global-banned** — 60%+ global ban rate, engine should assume they're often unavailable
3. **Kaze is the sleeper carry** — 75% WR across 20 picks, especially on Bounty/Heist maps
4. **Mode matters MORE than overall tier** — Byron is B-tier overall but a trap in most modes. Lou is "niche" but 70% WR in Hot Zone
5. **Gale is a massive trap pick** — most picked Brawl Ball brawler but 27.3% WR. Teams pick him for comfort, not results
6. **Grand Finals shift the meta** — Chester drops off, Kaze/Pierce/Lou become the kings
7. **Ban priority per mode:**
   - Brawl Ball: Crow > Otis > Chester
   - Gem Grab: Crow > Sandy > Emz
   - Knockout: Angelo > Gene > Leon
   - Hot Zone: Crow > Ziggy > Poco
   - Bounty: Mortis > Crow > Byron
   - Heist: Crow > Cordelius > Chuck
8. **Regional differences matter** — EA bans Glowbert like NA bans Crow. SA plays a different game entirely with Gus/Pearl/Bo
